WHY AFK

FOR CA?

Did you know a single gaming PC pulls the same energy as six households?

Imagine our combined impact, especially during peak times. As gamers, we have the power to influence change. The solution is simple: let those pixels rest and go AFK during Flex Alerts.
Our collective action will help stop outages, fuel a greener California so we can game on for
a sustainable future. Twitch giants are already in.

WHAT IS A FLEX ALERT?

Conserving energy is crucial. By using less during peak times, we can avoid outages and keep the power grid stable. Flex Alerts inform you when the grid is at capacity and encourage you to reduce energy use during those peak times. These alerts are especially important in the summer when electricity use spikes.

HOW?

When you are notified about a Flex Alert, seize the day to unplug from screens and engage in some off-the-wall passions like soap carving or achieve true zen in goat yoga, or even wrestle an octopus (good luck!)
